PRINCIPAL'S MESSAGE


Welcome to St Jerome's PU College, Bengaluru

“Education is not the learning of facts, but the training of mind to think” Says Albert Einstein. Here at St. Jerome's PU and Degree college we focus on the holistic growth of the students thus we take education beyond class rooms. Just marks and certificates are not enough to face life, so we train our students also to think creatively and conceptually. The aim of education, for Aristotle, was to teach intellectual and moral virtues side by side. We mould our students strong, by educating them to understand and utilize the failures constructively so as to face this competitive and challenging world effectively. “We cannot always build the future for our youth, but we can build our youth for the future”. These words of Franklin D. Roosevelt perfectly describe the vision of our institution. Our college is blessed with a highly qualified faculties consist of dedicated group of staff, a campus landscape that expands each year with improved facilities, thus it creates vibrant and significant opportunities for the students to learn and to lead. Quality is never an accident, it is always the result of high intention, sincere efforts, intelligent direction and skillful execution says Will A Foster. Welcome to St. Jerome's Come as a student and go back a responsible citizen to lead the world. Here we teach history thus tomorrow you make history. We are waiting for you.
